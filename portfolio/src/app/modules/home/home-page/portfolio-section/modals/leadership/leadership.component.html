Best ways to lead a team

Leading a small or rmedium size team is different then leading a huge team of 15+ members. We have to try to avoid long unneccessary meetings and all team members have to be involved in communication all the time.

As a team lead I like to have good communication and grerat relationship with every team member and I have to know all about thieir problems and chalanges they encounter.

Most important within a team is:

Great organization

Easy and quick communication

Daily sync between team members

Keep up with the progress

Find and resolve bottlenecks

If the team is huge (15+ members) then we have to split the team into two or more groups (5-10 members each group). In that way it is going to be easyer to menage team members and they will be more productive.

Benefits of having a smaller team is having shorther meetings and easyer and quicker communication within the team.
